## Summary
A Fermi arc is a surface-state locus in boundary momentum space whose endpoints lie at the projections of bulk Weyl nodes of opposite total charge.

## Scope
Lecture One surface-state discussion connected to bulk Weyl nodes.

## Regime
Boundary spectrum of a Weyl semimetal.

## Assumptions
- `A boundary geometry is specified.`
- `Bulk Weyl nodes project to distinct boundary momenta.`

## Representation
Boundary-state locus in the surface Brillouin zone.
