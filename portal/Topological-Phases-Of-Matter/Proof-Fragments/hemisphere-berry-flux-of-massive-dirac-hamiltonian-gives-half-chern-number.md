## Summary
The lower band of H = p_x sigma_x + p_y sigma_y + m sigma_z defines a map from the compactified momentum plane to one hemisphere of S^2, so its Berry flux is one half of the sphere degree and equals sign(m)/2.

## Scope
Geometric step behind the half-level induced Chern-Simons term in Lecture Three.

## Regime
Continuum 2+1-dimensional Dirac cone viewed as a two-band Berry bundle.

## Assumptions
- `The single-particle Hamiltonian is the continuum massive Dirac cone.`
- `The occupied band is the lower-energy band.`
