## Summary
If A and B obey AB = omega BA with omega^r = 1 primitive, then repeated action of A cycles the r eigenvalues of B and generates an r-dimensional irreducible representation.

## Scope
Representation-theoretic step behind the r-fold torus ground-state degeneracy.

## Regime
Torus Wilson-loop algebra in level-r abelian Chern-Simons theory.

## Assumptions
- `omega = exp(2 pi i / r) is a primitive r-th root of unity.`
- `B is diagonalizable on the physical Hilbert space.`
