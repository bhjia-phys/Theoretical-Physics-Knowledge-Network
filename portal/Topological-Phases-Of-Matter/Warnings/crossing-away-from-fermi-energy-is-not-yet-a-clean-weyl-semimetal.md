# Crossing Away From The Fermi Energy Is Not Yet A Clean Weyl Semimetal

- ID: `warning:crossing-away-from-fermi-energy-is-not-yet-a-clean-weyl-semimetal`

- Domain: `topological-phases-of-matter`

## Summary
A topological crossing can exist in a band structure without controlling the low-energy physics; to identify a clean Weyl semimetal, the crossing must be relevant near the Fermi energy.

## Scope
Lecture One distinction between formal crossings and low-energy semimetal phases.

## Regime
Band structures with crossings that may or may not sit near the chemical potential.
