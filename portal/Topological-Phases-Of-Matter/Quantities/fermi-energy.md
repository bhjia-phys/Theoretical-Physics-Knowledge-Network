## Summary
The Fermi energy is the reference energy that determines which band crossings control low-energy excitations; a crossing away from it does not automatically define a Weyl semimetal phase.

## Scope
Lecture One criterion for which crossings matter physically.

## Regime
Band structures near half-filling or another chosen filling.
